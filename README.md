# Small-Business-Sales-Profit-Analyzer
A  web application that analyzes sales data for small businesses, generating profit and loss statements, product performance rankings, and cash flow alerts from uploaded CSV files.

Features :

1 . Daily, weekly, and monthly profit & loss reports

2 . Product profitability rankings with margin analysis

3 . Cash flow monitoring with overstock and payment delay alerts

4 . Exportable PDF reports for banks and investors

5 . Offline operation using pure mathematical calculations

Target Users :

Small business owners, freelancers, and solopreneurs who need financial insights without complex accounting software
